About Malcolm Lismore

Malcolm Lismore, the talented freelance photographer, is a true nature enthusiast based on the stunning North West coast of Scotland. With a deep and unwavering passion for capturing the natural world in all its glory, Malcolm's lens has focused on the rugged and picturesque Scottish landscape, the rich and diverse wildlife that inhabits this region, and the graceful coastal birds that grace the shores.

Malcolm's portfolio is a testament to his profound connection with nature, and he has made a name for himself by offering his remarkable images to those who appreciate the wild beauty of Scotland. His work reflects not only his skill but also his love for the untamed, capturing the essence of the natural world in every shot.

Photography History

Malcolm Lismore's photography journey is a captivating narrative of a passionate individual who found his calling in capturing the beauty of the natural world and creating memorable moments through the lens. Here's a description of his remarkable photography journey:

Malcolm's photography odyssey began in the breathtaking landscapes of the North West coast of Scotland, where he was fortunate to grow up surrounded by the rugged and untamed beauty of the region. With each click of the shutter, he revealed the secrets of this wild terrain to the world.

As his portfolio expanded, so did his reputation.
